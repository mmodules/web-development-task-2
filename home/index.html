Welcome to CubingBlog

We are a community of speedcubers sharing tips, tricks, and solving techniques. On this website, you can explore the fascinating world of speedcubing with us.

Our website has quite a few interesting features. We have information about various cubing events and competitions. We also have a quiz section and a timer for practicing solves.

If you're interested in some opinion pieces, we also include a Top 3 list of the best cubing events. There, you can see our top picks for the most exciting cubing events of the year.

In the future, we will also be adding more content such as tutorials and a page with news on the top speedcubing highlights.

Feel free to explore the website and reach out to us through the Contact page if you have any questions or suggestions. Happy cubing!

3x3 Cube

The 3x3 is the most popular cube in the world. It's a great starting point for beginners and a challenge for experienced cubers.

This puzzle is a classic example of a twisty puzzle, where the goal is to align all the colors on each face of the cube.

Since it is the original twisty puzzle, it has inspired countless variations and innovations in the speedcubing community. It remains a cornerstone of the sport.
